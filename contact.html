Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers regarding parts, warranty, and our mechanical services.

Do you service all car brands?

Yes! With our 33 years of engineering mastery, we service all major luxury and consumer car companies including Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Honda, Toyota, Tata, BMW, Audi, Mercedes-Benz, and more.

Do you provide pickup & drop?

Absolutely. We offer convenient, safe vehicle pickup and drop-off services across the region. You can select this option directly when booking online.

How long does servicing take?

A standard general service or oil change takes about 2 to 4 hours. Major mechanical rebuilding or engine diagnostics can take 1 to 2 days. We always provide a clear time estimate beforehand.

Are genuine parts used?

Yes, we exclusively source and install 100% genuine OEM spare parts directly from certified distributors, ensuring your vehicle's safety, warranty, and resale value.

Can I book online?

Yes! You can reserve your workshop slot in real-time on our "Book Service" page, which integrates live slot verification and immediate booking log confirmation.

Do you provide warranty?

Yes, all our professional mechanical overhauls, diagnostic repairs, and premium spare parts come with a workshop warranty of up to 6 months.
