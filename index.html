Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium automobile workshop procedures, service timelines, and parts warranties.

What brands of vehicles do you specialize in?

We are a professional multi-brand luxury workshop. While we service everyday commuter brands like Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Honda, and Toyota with expert precision, our technicians are also specially trained and equipped with dedicated manufacturer-grade diagnostic scanners for premium European and luxury vehicles, including BMW, Audi, Mercedes-Benz, Porsche, and Jaguar.

Do you use genuine certified spare parts?

Absolutely. We have a strict zero-tolerance policy against counterfeit or sub-standard components. Every spare part, engine oil, filter, and electronic element installed at Star Auto Service is sourced directly from original manufacturers (OEM/OES) or authorized premium distributors, and carries its standard official replacement warranty.

How long does a general car service take?

A standard scheduled General Car Service (oil change, filter updates, and comprehensive 50-point diagnostic inspection) takes approximately 2 to 4 hours. If any complex issues are discovered that require parts replacements or deep mechanical calibration, our team (led by Papau Bhai or Iqbal Bhai) will immediately contact you with a detailed photo breakdown and a revised delivery timeline.

Do you offer emergency breakdown or roadside assistance?

Yes, we offer dedicated emergency breakdown support! If your vehicle fails to start or breaks down on the highway, you can call Papau Bhai (+91 9106770230) or Iqbal Bhai (+91 9909698474) directly. We will either dispatch a recovery technician to your location for on-the-spot minor repairs, or coordinate an emergency flatbed tow truck to transport your vehicle safely to our workshop.

Is there a pick-up and drop-off facility?

Yes. For our patrons' convenience, we provide safe pick-up and drop-off services for your vehicle across the city. Our professional drivers are fully insured and adhere to strict safety standards, ensuring your car arrives at our garage and returns back to your doorstep in pristine condition.
