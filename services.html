Client Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, direct answers to common questions about our operations, warranties, and premium repair protocols.

How long does a general service take?

A general car service usually takes between 3 to 5 hours. This includes our detailed multi-point safety sweep, filter replacements, fluid checks, adjustments, and a comprehensive final test run.

Do you use genuine spare parts?

Yes, we exclusively procure and install certified original OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and OES (Original Equipment Supplier) components. We never install substandard aftermarket duplicates.

Can I book online?

Absolutely! You can book your slot in under a minute via our Book Service page. Select your desired service category and fill in your vehicle details, and our managers will contact you immediately to lock in the slot.

Do you repair all car brands?

Yes, with over 33 years of professional engineering experience, our master mechanics Papau Bhai and Iqbal Bhai service and calibrate all major domestic and luxury brands—including Maruti, Hyundai, Tata, Honda, Mahindra, Toyota, Skoda, BMW, Audi, Mercedes, and Jaguar.

Is roadside assistance available?

Yes! We run a dedicated 24x7 emergency call-out team for immediate roadside help. If your car breaks down, simply call our hotline, and we will dispatch a rescue mechanic or arrange a rapid flatbed tow.

Do you provide warranty on repairs?

We stand fully behind our craftsmanship. All premium mechanical repairs come with a robust workmanship warranty, and all genuine OES/OEM parts carry original manufacturer warranties.
